**HR Attrition Dashboard**

This dashboard was developed in Tableau using an HR Excel dataset. Calculated fields such as Attrition Count and Attrition Rate were created to analyze employee turnover trends.

**Key Insights:**

1.R&D department had the highest attrition, while HR had the lowest.

2.Male employees showed higher attrition across all age groups compared to females.

3.The 25–34 age group experienced the highest attrition for both genders.

4.Employees with a Bachelor’s degree had the highest attrition rate.

5.Laboratory Technicians had the highest attrition among job roles.

6.Employees who traveled less frequently showed higher attrition.

The dashboard identifies key attrition patterns and highlights areas with higher employee turnover.
